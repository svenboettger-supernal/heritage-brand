The Mark

A seal, not a logo.

The wax-seal HS monogram demoted to a detail element.

The actual heritage-logo.webp file is the source of truth. Never redrawn as inline SVG, never recolored, never gradient-tinted, never set at hero scale. The seal is a 24 to 48px detail in section terminators, the footer, and the favicon. The page does the work, not the seal.

Clearspace

Reserve the cap height of the "H" in "Heritage" around the entire lockup. Nothing crosses that boundary.

Never alter

No stretching, rotating, recoloring, gradient tinting, drop-shadows, or containing shapes. The seal ships with the colors it has.

Typography

Two fonts, one mono.

Display serif for heading, sans for everything else, mono for fiduciary detail.

Tiempos Headline (Klim) does the heavy lifting for headings and the wordmark. Söhne (Klim) handles navigation, body, and dense data. If Klim licenses are unavailable, Fraunces and Inter are mandatory free fallbacks. JetBrains Mono carries account numbers, CUSIPs, and document references. Three display + body fonts in a Heritage surface is a redesign, not a variation.

Burgundy is identity

The wax-seal color. The single primary CTA per page. Link hover. Nothing else. Never a chart series, never a button default, never a paragraph color.

Bronze is hairline

1px structural rules and the section eyebrow only. Never a button fill, never a chart series, never a paragraph color. The picture frame, not the picture.

Reserved blue is state

Active, selected, focus, info. The moment reserved blue becomes decorative, its state meaning breaks everywhere else.

No warning hue

Zero use of hue 5 to 45 degrees at saturation greater than 55 percent. Warning-band colors do not appear on a fiduciary surface.

Visual Accents

Three motifs, no more.

The animated arc, the wax seal demoted to detail, and the 1px bronze hairlines.

One piece of motion ornament in the entire brand, the luminous arc, used once per page inside the navy hero panel. The wax-seal monogram is a 24 to 48px detail in section terminators and the footer. Top and bottom of the page are framed by 1px bronze hairlines. A fourth motif is a redesign.

House Rules

Do · Don't

Restraint over volume. Show, never claim.

Do

  • Default to the warm ivory ground. Cards sit on pure white. Dark panels are insets only.
  • Use the real heritage-logo.webp file. Never redraw the seal as inline SVG.
  • Use exactly two display + body fonts. Display serif plus sans. Mono is for fiduciary detail only.
  • Reserve Wax Burgundy for identity. The seal, one CTA per page, link hover.
  • Reserve Reserved Blue for state. Active, selected, focus, info.
  • Frame the page with 1px bronze hairlines, top and bottom.
  • Tabular figures in any dense data. Statements, position tables, stewardship counts.
  • Show the partner. Named, with credentials, real face, available light.
  • Use the animated luminous arc once per page, inside the navy hero panel.
  • Numbers beat adjectives. $X AUM, Y generations, Z years.

Don't

  • Don't make the page background dark. Fiduciary brands run on light grounds.
  • Don't use the burgundy as a chart series, a button default, a paragraph color, or a decorative fill.
  • Don't paint a button burgundy by default. Bronze-outline navy-text is the default.
  • Don't redraw the logo. Use the actual file.
  • Don't carry the thick 12px bottom bar from JetHQ. 1px bronze hairlines only.
  • Don't use stock wealth photography. Tarmac handshakes, sunset boardrooms, champagne flutes.
  • Don't write "we believe," "we are passionate about," "premier," "trusted," "world-class," "boutique," or "white-glove."
  • Don't use em dashes. Use commas, periods, or parentheses.
  • Don't animate the AUM. Static Tiempos. The number is supposed to feel old.
  • Don't visit hue 5 to 45 degrees at saturation greater than 55 percent. That is the warning band.
